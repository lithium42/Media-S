Facebook Page Publish - Detailed Setup Instructions

Facebook Page Publish publishes your posts to a facebook profile or page. To do so, it has to connect to facebook. This is done via a facebook application.

Step 1

You have to create your own facebook application.

This may require an authentification of your facebook account via a valid mobile phone number or a credit card.

Step 2

Go to your application's settings page and copy the application ID and the application secret to the approptiate option fields of the plugin.

To authorize your blog to be able to use your application, you have to fill in the URL (without path) and optionally the domain of your blog.

Step 3

You have to decide where your posts shall appear on facebook: user profile or page?

Publish to a page's wall

Go to your facebook page or create a new one where your blog posts shall appear. Copy the page ID to the appropriate option field of the plugin.

Confirm the plugin settings and don't forget to grant your blog permission to access your facebook page. To do so, simply click the grant permissions button on the plugin's settings page.
